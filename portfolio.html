Ski City

Ski City: INFO-I 211

This project was created for INFO-I211 and is a dynamic full stack web application. It integrates a front-end, back-end, database, and runs on a server. You are able to create and edit customers at a ski resort and check them in and out of the slopes and check in/out equipment from the resort's inventory.

  • Built with: Flask, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
  • Focuses: Boostrap design, usability, security, and real-world applications

Summit: Men's Mental Health App

Summit Prototype - INFO-I300

Summit was a prototype mobile app designed to break down the social stigma around men's mental health. It was made and iterated over the course of a semester and was one of my first steps into true tech design keeping in mind user experience and usability.

  • Built with: Figma
  • Focus: HCI/d Design and real world applications. Simulate the designing and iterative process of making a mobile application.
