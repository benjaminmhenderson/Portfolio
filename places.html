The Places I've Been

A lot of these places are ones I called home for a time. Whether I was climbing, camping, backpacking. With friends, family, or alone, these places drew me to them and I would return to any one of them in a heartbeat. But there are also so many more places that I have yet to see. Below are some notes on my favorites.

Grand Teton National Park, WY

I first fell in love with the Tetons in 2018, it was the first major destination on what became annual summer trips with my dad. I've since returned 4 times now and sport two tattoos of the entire outline of the range, split across both of my forearms.

Joshua Tree, CA

First visiting here in the summer of 2023 with my dad, I was in awe with the other worldly aspect of the desert in addition to world class climbing. I returned here with two of my friends in the spring of 2024 and climbed Igneous Ambiance V7.

Yosemite, CA

My first time here was again in the summer of 2023 with my dad, and I instantly knew it was not enough. I plan to return here for the summer of 2025 as an employee in the valley where I will spend 3 months climbing, hiking, and soaking in one of the most beautiful places on Earth.
