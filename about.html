About Me

General Climbing Travels Gallery
Photo by: Daisy Stricler
I aim to have passion in everything I pursue. From the climbing crag to the computer terminal I always want to be passionate about what I spend my time doing. Studying Informatics at Indiana University has helped me be flexible in my desires. I am choosing to focus and minor in Web Design as I love being able to walk away from my work with a tangible product to display. While doing this I am studying a second minor in Philosophy. I think it is imperative to know and understand different ways of thinking and applying those ways to the world of technology and life itself. I prefer to take my time in life but I'm not afraid of the unpredictable. Life is too short to speed through it.

Being born and raised in the great state of Indiana made me appreciate all there is that the world has to offer. I am eager to travel and move out to the western United States shortly after graduation, but I will never be able to forget where I'm from. I value my ability and willingness to try new things and think critically.

For years now I have been an avid climber, backpacker, traveller, gamer, and reader. These hobbies bring a balance to my life that allow me to enjoy almost any environment or space I am in and they allow me to continually connect with more and more people around the world. I want my life to be full of good places, good people, and good times.
