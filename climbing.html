Climbing

General Climbing Travels Gallery
Photo by: Andrew Parodi
Climbing became part of my life in my junior year of highschool. I tried it once in January of 2022, but did not start consistently going until March of that year. Ever since then it has been the center of my life. It has given friends, jobs, and helped me develop clear life goals after graduation. It has driven me to become more adventurous and helped me through many points of life. I wouldn't be where I am today if I had not tried out climbing that winter.
Climbing has also enabled my other creative passion to flourish. I have not had much time to dedicate to videography and photography yet but I have made a handful of projects.
